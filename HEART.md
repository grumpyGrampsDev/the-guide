# The Heart Behind This Guide

This guide was not written because I have arrived.

It was written because I am still learning.

Every page in this repository has been shaped by moments of discovery, conviction, encouragement, correction, and joy as God has faithfully met me through His Word. It is the journal of a student, not the textbook of a teacher.

My desire is not to tell others what to think.

My desire is to encourage others to look carefully at what God has already said.

If there is one posture I hope this guide cultivates, it is humility.

The Bible is not a puzzle to conquer.

It is God's self-revelation.

We do not stand over it as judges, deciding which parts we will accept. We come beneath it, allowing it to examine us, correct us, comfort us, and transform us.

I hope this guide encourages curiosity.

Not the curiosity that seeks novelty, but the kind that slows down long enough to ask good questions, notice repeated words, follow themes, appreciate context, and marvel at how beautifully Scripture fits together.

I hope it encourages patience.

Some passages become clear immediately.

Others take years.

Some questions receive satisfying answers.

Others remind us that finite people are reading the words of an infinite God.

That should not discourage us.

It should cultivate wonder.

Most of all, I hope this guide encourages faithfulness.

The measure of a fruitful Bible study is not how many notes were written or how much information was retained.

It is whether God's Word has been believed, obeyed, and treasured.

If these pages help someone become more impressed with this guide than with Christ, then they have missed their purpose.

If they instead help someone linger a little longer over God's Word, pray a little more honestly, trust Christ a little more deeply, or walk a little more faithfully, then they have accomplished exactly what I hoped they would.

So, wherever you are as you begin reading—

Whether you have walked with Christ for decades or are opening the Bible for the very first time—

Welcome.

Let's open God's Word together with humble hearts, attentive minds, and an expectation that He is faithful to accomplish His purposes through it.
