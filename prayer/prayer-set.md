# Daily Prayer Set

Prayer is not a formula.

These prayers are simple reminders of how to return to God throughout the day.

They are anchors:

- Remember God's grace.
- Seek His guidance.
- Offer your heart.
- Trust His work.

---

# One Sentence Anchor

Lord, give me light, guide my steps, form my heart, and bear Your fruit in me.

---

# Morning Prayer

## Orientation

Lord,

Thank You for another day.

Give me light.

Guide my steps.

Form my heart.

Bear Your fruit through me.

Help me walk faithfully today.

Amen.

---

## Remember

**Light**

Show me what You have for me today.

**Steps**

Help me walk faithfully.

**Heart**

Keep shaping me into Christ.

**Fruit**

Produce Your character through me.

---

# Throughout the Day

## Returning to God

Thank You.

Give me light.

Guide my next step.

---

These simple prayers are reminders that God is present throughout ordinary moments.

They help return my attention to Him.

---

# Evening Prayer

## Reflection

Lord,

Thank You for today.

Forgive where I wandered.

Grow what You planted.

Prepare tomorrow's step.

I rest in Your grace.

Amen.

---

## Remember

**Thank You**

Recognize God's goodness and faithfulness.

**Forgive Me**

Bring failures and wandering honestly before Him.

**Grow Me**

Trust that God continues His work.

**Grace**

Rest in what God has done.

---

God provides the light.

My calling is to walk faithfully in it.
