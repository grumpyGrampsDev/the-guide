# The Guide

> _Don't panic! Open your Bible._

A version-controlled companion for reading, studying, praying, and being formed by Scripture.

---

## What is this?

The Guide is a living collection of notes, principles, and practices that have helped me approach God's Word with greater humility, attentiveness, and faithfulness.

It is not intended to replace Scripture, but to point back to it. Every page exists to encourage a deeper engagement with the Bible itself.

This repository grows alongside my own walk with Christ. As I continue to learn, this guide will continue to develop.

---

## Who is it for?

This guide is primarily written for myself.

If it proves helpful to others, I am grateful. My hope is simply to share what God has taught me through His Word in a way that encourages others to open their Bibles, read carefully, and walk faithfully.

---

## Guiding Principles

- Scripture is the authority.
- Context matters.
- Read slowly.
- Pray often.
- Observe before interpreting.
- Seek understanding before application.
- Let Scripture interpret Scripture.
- Walk in the light God has given today.
